About Punith Gear RC Cars

Born from a lifelong passion for motorsport and precision engineering, Punith Gear RC Cars is India's premium destination for hobby-grade radio-controlled racing machines. Every model in our showroom is hand-assembled, dyno-tested, and track-tuned to deliver full-scale performance at scale.

From authentic F1 replicas to all-terrain off-road monsters and pro drift rigs, we build machines that don't just race — they dominate. Welcome to the showroom where speed meets craftsmanship.

  • Hand-assembled hobby-grade chassis
  • Brushless motors & LiPo racing batteries
  • 2.4 GHz proportional radio systems
  • Lifetime tuning support & spare parts
